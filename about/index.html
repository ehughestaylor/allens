About Us

Quality fit and service since 1906, Allen's Shoes has served as Southern New Jersey's number one destination for premium quality footwear. Allen's Shoes is known for having a variety of styles in wide, extra wide and large sizes to fit your feet.

We also have a professional staff to assist you with your purchases. Voted best shoe store in Atlantic County for the past seven years, we look forward to seeing you in our store for the best service and selection.
